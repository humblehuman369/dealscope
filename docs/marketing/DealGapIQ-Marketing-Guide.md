## How to Use This Guide

Lead headlines with **"Hunt. Score. Close."** Use **"15 seconds"** anytime you can — it crushes "instant" and "in minutes" because it has a number on it. Every web page, ad, and post should hit at least one of the three acts (hunt, score, or close). Pages that hit all three will convert the best.

When in doubt, ask: *does this copy sound like a calculator, or does it sound like a platform that finds the deal, scores it, and closes it?* If it sounds like the first one, rewrite it.
