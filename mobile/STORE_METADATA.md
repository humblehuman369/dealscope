## Description

**Is that property a good deal? Know in 60 seconds.**

DealGapIQ is the fastest way to analyze any investment property. Paste an address, and get an instant Verdict Score, deal gap analysis, and six investment strategy breakdowns — before you waste hours on a bad deal.

**How It Works:**
1. Enter any US property address
2. Get an instant IQ Verdict Score (0-100)
3. See your Target Buy Price and Deal Gap
4. Compare 6 investment strategies side-by-side
5. Adjust assumptions in Deal Maker
6. Download a pro-grade proforma or generate an LOI

**Key Features:**
• IQ Verdict Score — Composite deal quality score based on Deal Gap, Return Quality, Market Alignment, and Deal Probability
• Deal Gap Analysis — See exactly how much room to negotiate between list price and your target buy
• 6 Investment Strategies — Long-Term Rental, Short-Term Rental, BRRRR, Fix & Flip, House Hack, and Wholesale
• Deal Maker — Adjust financing, income, and expense assumptions with real-time metric recalculation
• DealVaultIQ — Save and track properties in your investment pipeline
• Proforma Export — Download investor-ready Excel and PDF reports
• LOI Generator — Create and share Letters of Intent
• Camera Scanner — Point at a property sign to start analysis

**Proprietary Metrics:**
• Income Value — Maximum price where cash flow equals zero
• Target Buy — Recommended purchase price (5% below Income Value)
• Deal Gap — Your negotiation advantage as a percentage

**Free Plan:** 5 analyses per month, 1 strategy
**Pro Plan:** Unlimited analyses, all 6 strategies, Deal Maker, proforma, LOI ($29/month with 7-day free trial)

Built for real estate investors who value speed, accuracy, and confidence in every deal decision.
