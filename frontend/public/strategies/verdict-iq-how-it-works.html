Understanding Breakeven Price

The maximum price you can pay and still break even on monthly cash flow.

Breakeven is calculated using YOUR assumptions:

Down payment %
Interest rate
Loan term
Vacancy rate
Management fees
Maintenance %

At breakeven: Monthly rental income exactly covers all expenses (mortgage, taxes, insurance, vacancy, maintenance, management). Any price below breakeven = positive cash flow.

Based on LTR (Long-Term Rental) revenue model using estimated market rent.

1. Deal Gap %

The discount needed from asking price to reach your breakeven price.

Deal Gap = (List Price or Market Value − Breakeven) / List Price or Market Value × 100

Breakeven is the maximum price where monthly cash flow = $0, calculated using YOUR financing terms (down payment, interest rate, vacancy, etc.).

For listed properties, List Price is used. For off-market properties, Market Value (Zestimate) is used.
