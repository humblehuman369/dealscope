## LinkedIn-specific patterns

- **Hook in line 1.** All three posts open with the contrarian observation, not the conclusion.
- **First-comment URL strategy.** LinkedIn de-prioritizes posts with outbound links in the body. All three posts move the URL to a first comment posted by you.
- **No emojis except as bullet markers.** Sparingly used (✶, ❌, 1️⃣) for visual rhythm.
- **2,000-char ceiling.** Each post is sized to be visible in feed without "see more" clipping the hook.

## X-specific patterns

- **Tweet 1 = standalone hook.** Reads complete on its own; the 🧵 invites depth.
- **Each tweet ≤280 chars.** Verified before drafting.
- **Final tweet contains the link + manifesto motif.** Algorithm rewards the thread length; the link is the conversion hook.
- **Cadence note:** brand template targets 5–7 X posts/week. With 3 threads ≈ 22 tweets, plus standalone manifesto/glossary drops, you have enough material in this batch alone for 3+ weeks of X cadence.

## YouTube-specific patterns

- **Description optimized for SEO + YouTube Discover.** Chapters with timestamps, hashtags grouped at the bottom, links in the resources section.
- **Brad-signed where appropriate** (F3 description signs explicitly; G1 and B4 are tool-explainer videos and stay company-voiced).
- **Resources section drives crosslinking** between blog/glossary/verdict tool — same SEO topic-cluster logic as the on-domain internal links.
